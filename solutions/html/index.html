Step into ACES

Joining ACES means becoming part of a diverse team of over 3,300 engineers, scientists, and professionals, all committed to delivering innovative and reliable engineering solutions. With a strong presence across the Middle East and the USA, ACES offers a collaborative, growth-oriented environment backed by cutting-edge technology and over 40 years of expertise. Our focus on quality, backed by ISO certifications and numerous awards, ensures that employees are part of a forward-thinking company dedicated to excellence, professional development, and contributing to impactful projects worldwide.

Embracing Differences

At ACES, we embrace differences and cultivate an inclusive culture that values every individual's unique perspectives and experiences. Our commitment to diversity drives us to create a workplace where everyone feels welcomed, respected, and empowered to contribute.

Key Aspects of Our Inclusive Culture:

  • Diverse Workforce: We proudly bring together professionals from various backgrounds, cultures, and expertise, enriching our teams with a wealth of knowledge and insights.
  • Collaborative Environment: We encourage open dialogue and collaboration, where team members are empowered to share their ideas and viewpoints without hesitation, fostering creativity and innovation.
  • Equal Opportunities: We are dedicated to providing equal opportunities for growth and development, ensuring that all employees have access to the resources and support they need to succeed.
  • Training and Awareness: We prioritize ongoing training and awareness programs that educate our staff on the importance of diversity and inclusion, helping to build a culture of understanding and respect.
  • Celebrating Diversity: Through various initiatives, events, and recognition programs, we celebrate the diverse backgrounds and achievements of our employees, creating a vibrant workplace where everyone can thrive.

At ACES, we believe that our differences are our greatest strength, driving us to innovate and excel in our mission to provide specialized engineering services worldwide. Join us in shaping a future that values inclusivity and embraces the richness of diversity.
